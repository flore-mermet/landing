What's Ell

An inclusive, ethical and responsible brand. Created in 2023, Ell offers women the possibility to find in their wardrobe timeless clothes with multiple uses. The pieces mix jewellery and fabrics that are interchangeable. We want to create a brand that fits everyone while limiting the effects of fast fashion.
